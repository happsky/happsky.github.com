Biography (CV)

I am currently a research assistant of Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I got my bachelor degree from South China University of Technology in major of Electronic Engineering. After that, I worked in XI'AN Institute of Optics and Precision Mechanics, Chinese Academy of Sciences as a research assistant.

 

I am interested in feature extraction, feature representation, index construction and there application to content-based image retrieval. I built an image retrieval website demo based on Oxford 5K dataset. Meanwhile, I am recently learning deep neural network and I am using CNN (convolution neural network) for blur classification and processing tasks.
